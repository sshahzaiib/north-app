North

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

North is an anti-anxiety family location app. It helps the people you choose — your “circle” — see that you’re safe through a shared map and gentle place-based arrival alerts, without surveillance or noise. This Privacy Policy explains what information North collects, how it is used and shared, and the choices you have.

The short version.
  • North collects your location (including in the background) so your circle can see your presence and receive arrival alerts.
  • Your information is shared only with the circle members you choose and the service providers that run the app.
  • We do not sell your data, and North contains no advertising.
  • You can revoke permissions or delete your account at any time.

1. Who we are

North (“North”, “we”, “us”) is the provider of the North mobile app and is the controller of the personal information described in this policy. If you have any questions, contact us at privacy@yourdomain.com.

2. Information we collect

Account information

  • Your email address, used to sign you in with a secure “magic link” (we do not store a password).
  • Your first name and a chosen avatar color and initial.
  • An optional phone number you may add to your profile.

Location information

  • With your permission, North collects your device’s precise location both while you are using the app and in the background.
  • Location is used to display your live presence to your circle and to detect when you arrive at or leave a saved place.
  • We may derive a coarse “in transit” status from your device’s movement.

Saved places

  • The places you designate as Smart Places (for example Home or Work), including their names and coordinates.

Notifications and device signals

  • A push notification token, so we can deliver alerts such as arrivals, daily check-ins, and (on Premium) routine and low-battery alerts.
  • Limited device signals such as battery level, used only to power low-battery alerts.

Subscription information

  • If you subscribe to North Premium, your purchase is processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play and managed through RevenueCat. We receive your subscription status and a pseudonymous app-user identifier — not your full payment-card details.

Diagnostic information

  • Basic, app-generated logs used to keep the service reliable and to diagnose problems.

3. How we use your information

  • To show your presence on the shared map and operate your circle.
  • To send the alerts and check-ins you and your circle rely on.
  • To manage your subscription and entitlements.
  • To secure the service, prevent abuse, and fix problems.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Background location — prominent disclosure

North collects location data to enable live presence sharing with your circle and place-based arrival alerts even when the app is closed or not in use. You can turn this off at any time in your device settings; doing so will disable presence sharing and arrival alerts.

5. How we share information

With your circle

The core purpose of North is to share your presence with the circle members you choose. Those members can see your location, your presence status, and your arrivals at saved places for as long as you are part of the circle. You control your participation and can leave a circle or stop sharing at any time.

With service providers

We use a small number of trusted providers who process data on our behalf, under contract and only as needed to run North:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, real-time sync, and storage.
  • Mapbox — map rendering and related services.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management and validation.
  • Expo — delivery of push notifications to your device.
  • Apple and Google — app distribution and payment processing.

For legal reasons

We may disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the service.

What we never do

We do not sell your personal information, and North does not show advertising or share your data with advertisers.

6. Data retention

We keep your account information for as long as your account is active. Location updates are retained only for the limited time needed to provide presence and history features and are then removed or aggregated. When you delete your account, we delete your personal information from our active systems within a reasonable period, except where we must retain it to comply with the law.

7. Your rights and choices

  • Permissions: you can revoke location or notification access at any time in your device settings. Some features will stop working without them.
  • Access and correction: you can view and edit your profile information in the app.
  • Account deletion: you can delete your account and associated personal data from within the app, or by contacting us at privacy@yourdomain.com.
  • Regional rights: depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (such as access, portability, or objection) under laws like the GDPR or CCPA. Contact us to exercise them.

8. Security

We protect your information with encryption in transit and at rest. Sensitive keys are stored in your device’s secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore), and access to circle data is restricted by row-level security so that only members of a circle can see one another’s information. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information using industry-standard safeguards.

9. Children’s privacy

North is a family app, but accounts are intended for people aged 16 and over (or the age of digital consent in your country). A parent or legal guardian may permit a child to participate in a family circle; if you do so, you are responsible for that child’s information and for obtaining any consent required by law. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us information without the proper consent, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International data transfers

North relies on cloud infrastructure that may store and process your information in countries other than the one in which you live. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

12. Contact us

Questions or requests about this policy or your information? Email us at privacy@yourdomain.com.
